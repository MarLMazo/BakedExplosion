About us

At Baked Explosion our main goal is to make customer happy. People of Toronto are enjoying our shop since last 23 years.

our donuts are voted one of the best donuts in Canada. Plus our menu items are very affordable for everyone. Please visit us and try our delicious bake food.

Our History

One day Sukhdeep, Krishna, Marl, Amit and Yash decided to buy a property for their business. Initially there were thinking about starting a vegetarian restaurant, as there were very less good vegetarian restaurant in the city.

But they realised there were not any bakery nearby the property they bought. So they decided to start a bakery. They introduced very few item when the store first opened in 1996. They started the store with selling Donuts, cookies and Muffin. Later on, as the store keep getting popular they started adding more and more things.

We have got successful only because of our wonderful customers, many of whom are like family. If you meet any of our founder, they will always welcome you with a friendly smile and big hello. We know many of customers name and we would love to know yours too, so, what are you waiting for?

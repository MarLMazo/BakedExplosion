Job Opportunities

  • Cake Decorator : Part Time

    Key Responsibilities

    We are looking for enthusiastic creative and experienced cake decorators to join our growing team. The applicant must be able to slice, cut, decorate, fill and finish cakes.Decorate & slice cakes for retail sale.Keep track of inventory/cake stock.Think quickly to resolve any unexpected issues.Ensuring kitchen cleanliness, including washing dishes, mopping and sweeping

    Qualifications

    Must know how to mask a cake.Able to work with buttercream icing and fondant is a must.Attention to detail and able to work in a team atmosphere.Must be energetic, passionate about Pastry and Cakes.Must have a good eye for color and its combinations.Must have a positive attitude, enjoys challenges and is a fast learner.

  • Catering Coordinator : Full Time

    Key Responsibilities

    Facilitates the guest ordering experience and suggests appropriate packages.Manages the flow of catering business within the cafe.Makes catering fit seamlessly into bakery-cafe operations.Prepares catering orders that are 100% accurate and on time

    Qualifications

    Ability to work in a fast paced environment.Team-oriented, adaptable, dependable, and strong work ethic.Ability to communicate effectively with guests and team members.Ability to work weekends and holidays.Must be at least 18 years of age
